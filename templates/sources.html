DB sources

Volumio creates and updates its music database scanning the content of the following source directories:

  • NAS
    Where all network mounts fit (SAMBA or NFS)
  • USB
    The content of the USB drive, if plugged (FAT32 or NTFS)
  • RAM
    The content of the RAMdisk, upload your files here and enjoy RamPlay Heaven.
